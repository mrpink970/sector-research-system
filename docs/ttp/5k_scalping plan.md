## Pre-Market Data

Pre-market data (before 9:30 AM ET) is collected and logged but **not** traded. It is used for:

- Seeing early direction
- Identifying support/resistance levels
- Gauging volatility

**No trades before 9:45 AM ET.**

## Step-by-Step Execution

### Before Market Open

**Step 1: Check Your Email**

- Do you have a **GREEN DAY** email?
- ✅ YES → Proceed to Step 2
- ❌ NO → Do not trade. Stop.

### Market Open (9:30 AM ET)

**Step 2: Wait**

- **Do NOT place any trades before 9:45 AM ET**
- Let the market show its direction first

### Entry Window (9:45 AM - 10:30 AM ET)

**Step 3: Enter the Trade**

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Action | Buy |
| Ticker | SOXL |
| Shares | 15 |
| Trailing Stop | **$25** (enter in ticks: ~167 ticks at $250) |
| Take Profit | **$45** (enter in dollars) |

**Place the order.**

**Step 4: Walk Away**

- Do not watch the trade tick by tick
- Let the trailing stop and take profit do their job
- Check back only when you get an alert

### After Each Trade

**Step 5: Track Your Progress**

| Metric | How to Track |
|--------|--------------|
| Daily losses | Add up closed losses |
| Total profit | Check TTP dashboard |
| Trades completed | Count closed trades |
| Profit days ($25+) | Track separately |

**Step 6: Check Daily Losses**

| Total Losses Today | Action |
|--------------------|--------|
| $0 - $50 | ✅ Continue trading |
| $50 - $75 | ⚠️ Be careful |
| $75 - $100 | 🔴 Stop trading for the day |
| $100+ | 🔴 Account will pause automatically |

### End of Day

**Step 7: Check Daily Profit**

| Total Profit Today | Action |
|--------------------|--------|
| $25 or more | ✅ Mark as a profit day (need 3 total) |
| Less than $25 | ⏳ Keep trying tomorrow |
