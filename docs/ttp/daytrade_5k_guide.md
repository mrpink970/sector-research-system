# TTP Day Trade Flex Beginner 5K Guide

**Account Type:** Day Trade FLEX - Beginner ($5,000)
**Time Limit:** Unlimited

## Your Goal

Start with $5,000. Make $300 profit (6% target). Complete at least 10 trades. Then request review.

---

## The Rules

### Profit Target: 6% ($300)

- You need $300 total profit to pass

### Daily Pause: 2% ($100)

- You cannot lose more than $100 in realized losses in a single day
- If you hit $100 in losses, your account is **paused** until the next trading day
- This does **NOT** fail your evaluation — you can continue tomorrow

### Max Loss: 4% ($200)

- Your account cannot drop below **$4,800** (starting balance of $5,000 minus $200)
- This is a **static** drawdown — the floor does not move up with your profits
- If your account balance hits $4,800 at any time → ❌ **Your evaluation fails immediately**

### Minimum Positions: 10

- You need at least 10 completed trades before requesting review

### Minimum Trade Duration: 30 Seconds

- There must be at least 30 seconds between buy and sell orders for the same ticker

### Minimum Profit Per Trade: $0.10 per share

- Every profitable trade must make at least $0.10 per share
- A profitable trade with less than $0.10 per share does NOT count toward your profit target

### Consistency Rule: 50% of Total Profit ($300)

- Your largest trade cannot account for more than **50% of your $300 profit target** ($150)
- If any single trade makes more than $150, you will need to earn additional profit so that trade becomes less than 50% of your total

**How it works:**
- If your largest trade is $160, you need total profit of at least $320 ($160 ÷ 0.50)
- Since the profit target is $300, a trade over $150 means you must exceed the $300 target

**Example:**
- Largest trade $140 → ✅ Under $150, works within the $300 target
- Largest trade $160 → Need $320 total profit ($20 over the target) before requesting review
- Largest trade $200 → Need $400 total profit ($100 over the target) before requesting review

### Consistency Rule (Profit Days): Minimum 3 Days with 0.5%+ Profit

- You need at least 3 days where your total profit for that day exceeds 0.5% of your starting balance
- For a $5,000 account, 0.5% = **$25 profit in a single day**
- You must have at least 3 separate days where you make $25 or more

### Cash Out Rules: Minimum 14 Days Withdrawal Period

- After requesting payout, you must wait 14 days before withdrawing funds

### Prohibited Practices

- No holding overnight (Day Trade account closes positions before market close)
- No trading around earnings (will be liquidated)
- No arbitrage or latency-based strategies

---

## What You Must Do to Pass

### Position Sizing

- Use as many shares as your buying power allows without exceeding it
- As SOXL (or whatever ticker you are trading) increases in price, share count will need to decrease
- If the ticker decreases in value, share count can increase

### Trade Only on Green Days

- Only trade when you receive a GREEN DAY email from your signal system
- No Green Day = No trade

### Always Use a Trailing Stop

- Always enter a trailing stop when placing your order
- Always enter a take profit price when placing your order
- You can sell manually before the take profit price if you want to lock in gains early
- You can exit manually before the trailing stop hits if the trade starts going against you

### Trading Hours (General Guideline)

- These are general guidelines, not hard rules
- Some days all hours are good for trading, other days are not
- Market opens at **9:30 AM ET**
- **Wait for market direction before entering a trade**
- **Preferably no trades before 9:45 - 9:50 AM ET** (let the market establish direction)
- **9:45 - 10:30 AM ET** — Primary trading window (highest liquidity)
- **10:30 AM - 1:00 PM ET** — Often flat, but can be good some days
- **1:00 - 3:30 PM ET** — Secondary window (lower confidence)
- **Day Trade accounts must be flat by market close** (positions will be liquidated)

### Know When to Stop Each Day

- 0-1 losses → Keep trading
- 2 losses → Stop for the day (you are near daily pause)

### Track Your Profit Days

- You need at least 3 days with $25+ profit
- These can be from one trade or multiple trades in the same day
- The days do not need to be consecutive
