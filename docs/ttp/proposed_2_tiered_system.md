# Proposed 2-Tier Trading System - For Future Consideration

## Current System (Tier 1 Only)

The current TTP system follows strict rules:

- **Green Day** (Day Return ≥ 0.5%, RSI ≥ 50, above MA20) = Trade signal
- **Red Day** (Day Return < 0) = No trade
- Everyone follows the same rules

This system is proven and will remain the primary trading method.

---

## Proposed Addition: Tier 2 (Advanced Trades)

### Who This Is For

- Experienced traders only
- Those who monitor real-time charts (not just email alerts)
- Those who accept higher risk for more opportunities
- **Not for beginners**

### When Tier 2 Would Trigger

Tier 2 would consider trades on **Red Days** when a bounce setup forms:

| Condition | Requirement | How to Check |
|-----------|-------------|--------------|
| RSI overshoot | RSI was below 30 in the last 2 hours | Chart or indicator |
| RSI recovery | RSI now rising from low | Chart or indicator |
| Price vs VWAP | Price above VWAP | Chart or indicator |
| Price structure | Higher lows on 15-min chart | Visual chart check |
| Price structure | Higher highs on 15-min chart | Visual chart check |

### Trade Parameters (Day Trade Only)

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Position Size | 2 shares (same as standard) |
| Stop Loss | 2% below entry |
| Take Profit | 3-4% (tighter than standard 6%) |
| Max Hold Time | 3:45 PM ET (no overnight) |
| Trade Type | Day trade only (not swing) |

### Example Trade Plan

Entry: Current market price
Stop Loss: Entry - 2% (e.g., $560 → $549)
Take Profit: Entry + 3-4% (e.g., $560 → $577-582)
Risk/Reward: Approximately 1:1.5 to 1:2
Close By: 3:45 PM ET

## Risks of Adding Tier 2

| Risk | Description | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|------------|
| Lower win rate | 50-55% vs 60-65% for standard | Acceptable if risk/reward is good |
| Counter-trend trading | Buying during downtrend | Tight stops, smaller targets |
| Requires real-time monitoring | Email alone insufficient | Trader must watch charts |
| Not for beginners | Higher complexity | Clear warnings in email |
| False signals | Bounce may fail | Hard stop at 2% |

---

## Benefits of Adding Tier 2

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| More opportunities | Trade on red days, not just green |
| Better use of capital | Enter during oversold conditions |
| Flexibility | Adapt to different market conditions |
| Learning | Develop bounce trading skills |

## Decision Criteria

Before implementing Tier 2, the following should be considered:

1. Current system must prove profitable first (pass TTP evaluation)
2. Gather real-world data on bounce setups (log them, track results)
3. Review after 10-20 bounce setups (win rate, risk/reward)
4. Decide based on evidence, not emotion

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|

Tier 2 trades are optional. No trader is required to take them. Those who do accept full responsibility for their outcomes.
